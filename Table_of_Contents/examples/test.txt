# Introduction to Car Maintenance
## Importance of Regular Maintenance
Proper maintenance ensures safety, reliability, and longevity of your vehicle.
# Basic Maintenance Tasks
## Fluid Checks and Replacements
Check and replace engine oil, coolant, and other fluids as recommended.
## Tire Maintenance
Monitor tire pressure, tread depth, and rotate tires regularly.
# Advanced Maintenance
## Brake System
Inspect and replace brake pads and rotors as needed.
## Engine and Transmission
Perform tune-ups, replace filters, and check for leaks or worn components.
# Seasonal Maintenance
## Summer Preparation
Check the air conditioning system and prepare for hot weather conditions.
## Winter Preparation
Equip your vehicle with appropriate tires and check the heating system.
